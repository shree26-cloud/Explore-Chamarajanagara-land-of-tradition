Chamarajeshwara Temple

A historic Shiva temple located in the heart of Chamarajanagar city.

About

Chamarajeshwara Temple is one of the oldest and most famous temples in Chamarajanagar District. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is known for its beautiful Dravidian architecture, peaceful atmosphere and religious importance. Thousands of devotees visit the temple throughout the year, especially during Maha Shivaratri and other Hindu festivals.
